vpkg

(v package)

An alternative package manager written on V, for V.

Download

Bringing the best of dependency management on V.

Decentralized.

Download and use packages from other sources aside from VPM and vpkg registry.

Easy to use.

Set-up, use, and master the commands of vpkg CLI within minutes.

Fast.

vpkg can be run from the low-spec PC to the fastest supercomputers.

Interoperable.

It supports v.mod, and .vpm.json for reading package manifests and managing dependencies.

Light.

Weighs only at < 200kb so it can be used in devices with tight storage or in low network conditions.

Reliable.

vpkg uses a lockfile mechanism to ensure that all your dependencies work across all of your machines.

vpkg registry

An alternative package registry for managing and publishing your V modules. It's free, simple, and straightforward to use.
